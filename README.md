# Dark Theory Labs

### Researching How Intelligence Perceives, Reasons, and Acts

Dark Theory Labs is an AI research lab and product studio focused on building the next generation of intelligence infrastructure. We explore how intelligent systems perceive information, reason through complex problems, and take meaningful actions across digital environments.

Our mission is to create AI systems with persistent memory, contextual awareness, advanced retrieval, and long-term reasoning capabilities that move beyond traditional stateless models.

---

## Vision

Modern AI can generate impressive outputs, but most systems remain limited by fragmented context, shallow memory, and short-term interactions.

Dark Theory Labs is building infrastructure that enables intelligence to:

- Remember across sessions
- Retrieve relevant knowledge dynamically
- Understand evolving context
- Reason over long time horizons
- Act autonomously within defined environments

We believe the future belongs to AI systems that can continuously learn, adapt, and operate with persistent understanding.

---

## Research Areas

### Memory Systems

Developing architectures that allow AI to retain, organize, and utilize information across interactions.

### Retrieval Infrastructure

Building scalable retrieval systems that connect intelligence with relevant knowledge at the right time.

### Context Engineering

Designing frameworks that improve contextual awareness, state management, and reasoning quality.

### Agent Architectures

Researching autonomous systems capable of planning, execution, and decision-making.

### Knowledge Graphs

Exploring structured memory and semantic relationships for advanced reasoning.

### Long-Term Intelligence

Creating systems that accumulate understanding instead of starting from scratch every session.
